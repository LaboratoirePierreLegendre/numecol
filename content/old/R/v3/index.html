What is the R Package?

 The R Package is a collection of small programs for multivariate and spatial analysis. Originally developed for ecologists, it has found many uses throughout the scientific community. It is highly recommended that you download the accompanying documentation, in either Macintosh Self-reading (just double-click!), PostScript, or MacWrite II format. If you do not have a Macintosh computer, please see our emulation page for answers to frequently asked questions.
